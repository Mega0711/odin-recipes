Lasagna

Description

pasta in the form of broad often ruffled ribbons. a baked dish containing layers of boiled lasagna, and usually cheese, a seasoned sauce of tomatoes, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1-1/4 Pounds bulk italian sausage
  • 3/4 Pound ground beef
  • 1 Medium onion, diced
  • 2/3 Cup water

Steps By Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in dish
  2. Make a layer of coocked lasagna noodles
  3. Spread and even layer of the beef
  4. Throw the water
  5. Repeat those layer two times
